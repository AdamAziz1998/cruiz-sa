Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on the app?

You can create an account by clicking on "create account" then entering your personal information (such as full name, phone number, and email).

Can I sign up using my Google account?

Yes, you can sign up with your google account.

Is my personal information safe on the platform?

Yes. All personal data is protected under privacy and data security regulations.

Can I change my registered phone number or email address?

Yes. You can update your contact details from your account settings. A verification step may be required.

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click “Forgot Password” on the login screen. You'll receive a verification code via email to create a new password.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact customer service through the live chat feature in the app or through a phone call by clicking on contact customer service.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Follow the “Forgot Password” process on the login screen to reset it securely.

How can I update my personal information?

Go to your account settings in the app, where you can edit details such as your name, address, and contact information.

Can I have multiple accounts?

Each user is only allowed one account per person for compliance purposes.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your account settings then click on delete my account. Your information will be stored even after you delete your account for safety purposes.

How do I create a host account?

Download the Cruiz host app then follow the registration steps to creating the account by submitting the required documents and completing the host verification process within the app.

What documents are required to verify my account as a host?

You will need to submit a valid Saudi ID, vehicle registration and insurance documents.

How long does it take to get verified as a host?

It will take between 2 to 3 business days to verify the account.

Can I switch my account from guest to host?

You can not switch the account but you can create a new account in Cruiz Host app and just provide additional documents for the verification process.

What happens if my account verification is denied?

You'll receive an email with the reason for rejection. You can correct the issue (e.g. submit clearer documents) and reapply.

Can I register as a host if I don't live in Saudi Arabia?

No. The App currently only functions in Saudi Arabia

How can I verify my vehicle ownership?

By uploading your vehicle's registration document under your name or your company's name.

What happens if I change my phone number or email?

You can update this information in your account settings. A verification process will follow to confirm the change.

How do I list my vehicle on the platform?

Once verified as a host, you can list your vehicle by clicking on add vehicle on my fleet page. You would then have to enter the vehicle details (make, model, year, photos).

What documents are required to list my vehicle?

vehicle registration, Insurance document and an inspection certificate if your vehicle make is 3 years old or more.

Are there any mileage restrictions?

The minimum limit for the maximum mileage selected by the host is 100km

Can I list more than one vehicle?

You are only allowed to list one vehicle as an individual according to the regulations of the transport general authority.

Can I set my own rental price?

Yes, you can set your own price within the acceptable range for the vehicle model

Is there a minimum rental period for my vehicle?

The minimum rental period is one day.

How do I update my vehicle's availability?

You can manage availability using the calendar feature within the vehicle settings to block out days or open new dates for booking.

Can I unlist my vehicle temporarily without deleting its information?

Yes, you can unlist from the vehicle settings.

How is the rental price calculated?

The rental price is calculated by using the rental rate (set by the host) + VAT + platform service fee + optional add-ons (eg. vehicle delivery service).

Can I offer discounts for long-term bookings?

You can offer a weekly or monthly discount fee through the vehicle settings.

Are there extra charges for premium features like GPS or child seats?

No, you cannot add extra charges to vehicle. However, having these premium features with the vehicle would increase the rate at which the vehicle is rented.

When do I receive payment after rental?

Once the guest confirms the booking and pays for the fees, the payment will be held by cruiz for 1 week or until the rent period starts whichever one comes first then you will receive the payment automatically.

Can I change my payment method?

You can update your preferred payment method in your account settings.

Are there any platform service fees?

Yes. The platform will add 15% service fee to each completed booking.

How do I issue a refund to a guest?

The host cannot refund the guest. If the guest encounters any trouble the customer service team will resolve their issue.

What happens if a guest cancels their booking?

If the guest cancels the booking within the cancellation period set by the host, the full amount will be refunded. If the guest cancels the booking after the period set by the host, only part of the amount will be refunded.

Can I charge additional fees for extra mileage?

Yes, you can charge for extra mileage but the price for every extra kilometer is set by Cruiz.

What happens if I receive a late payment?

Payment is collected from the guest upfront by the platform, you may need to check with your bank if the payment took longer than a week.

How does VAT affect my earnings?

The VAT is a tax put on goods by the Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority. It will be added to the rental price, it will not be taken from your earnings.

How do I report damage caused by a renter?

You should report damages through the app or customer service as soon as the rental ends. You'll need to provide photo evidence and a damage report.

Who is responsible for insurance claims?

Your vehicle should be insured by an insurance company; any claims should be directed to them.

What happens if the guest is involved in an accident?

The guest should report the accident immediately. You will be notified, and the platform will guide both parties through the next steps.

What happens if the guest violates traffic laws while using my vehicle?

Once the guest picks up the vehicle, it will be automatically delegated to the guest through Absher. Therefore, if there are any traffic violations during the rental period, the guest will be fined.

How do I accept or decline a booking request?

You'll receive a notification through the app. You can accept or reject it through your dashboard.

Can I set restrictions on who can book my vehicle?

You cannot restrict the guests.

What should I do if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel from your dashboard, but frequent cancellations may affect your host rating. Always try to cancel with advance notice.

Will I be penalized for canceling a booking?

What happens if a guest returns my vehicle late?

You'll be compensated for late returns.
