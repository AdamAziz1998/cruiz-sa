Community Guidelines

Cruiz was created to empower individuals and communities through smart mobility. By sharing cars rather than owning more vehicles, we can reduce congestion, enhance accessibility and decrease the environmental footprint of transportation. These guidelines help us build a trusted, inclusive and sustainable community that aligns with Saudi Vision 2030's goals of smarter cities and economic diversification.

  • Abide by the law: All users must obey local traffic laws and regulations. Peer-to-peer car-sharing thrives when participants respect road rules and legal requirements. Cruiz's partners emphasize innovation in cooperation with insurance providers and regulators to support economic growth and Vision 2030.
  • Treat others with respect: Discrimination, harassment, or abusive behaviour has no place on Cruiz. We expect hosts and guests to communicate courteously and resolve issues in good faith. Car-sharing is a community endeavour—mutual respect makes it work.
  • Provide honest listings: Hosts should accurately describe and photograph their vehicles so guests know what they're booking. Cruiz lets guests filter by price, location and features; clear listings help everyone find the right match.
  • Maintain your vehicle: Hosts must keep their cars in safe, roadworthy condition. Because platforms like Cruiz are designed to enable individuals to earn additional income, owners have a responsibility to ensure vehicles meet safety standards.
  • Comprehensive protection: Cruiz trips include comprehensive insurance. Hosts and guests must not circumvent these policies. Peer-to-peer car-sharing programs in Saudi Arabia offer customised insurance solutions in partnership with insurers, so always verify that coverage is active before and during each trip.
  • Report incidents promptly: If an accident or mechanical issue occurs, contact Cruiz support and the appropriate authorities immediately. Prompt reporting allows us to assist quickly and ensure everyone's safety.
  • Prepare the car: Deliver the vehicle clean, fueled (or charged) and ready for use. While Cruiz makes bookings quick and easy, hosts should make sure the car meets the description and is fully functional.
  • Verify eligibility: Ensure that the guest has a valid driver's licence and meets Cruiz's eligibility requirements. Peer-to-peer car-sharing platforms rely on trust and proper verification.
  • Be punctual: Honour agreed pickup and drop-off times. Efficient scheduling helps maintain a seamless, multimodal transport system.
  • No hidden listings: Only list vehicles that are not simultaneously advertised on other rental platforms. Hosts must comply with Cruiz policies and local regulations when offering their cars.
  • Respect the vehicle: Treat the car as if it were your own. Do not tamper with, misuse or neglect the vehicle. Shared mobility helps reduce congestion and environmental impact—misuse undermines these benefits.
  • Return on time: Bring the car back at the agreed time and location, with the same level of fuel or charge. Timely returns help ensure efficient use of shared vehicles.
  • Keep it clean: Remove personal belongings and trash before returning the car. While normal dirt from weather and travel is expected, excessive mess may result in additional fees.
  • Use authorized drivers only: Only the person who booked the car and has been verified by Cruiz may drive it. Sharing the car with unauthorized drivers violates our policies and insurance agreements.
Cruiz supports Saudi Arabia's ambitions for sustainable urban mobility. The NEOM mobility strategy demonstrates how shared mobility, automation and electrification can create an efficient, multimodal transport system. By participating in Cruiz and following these guidelines, you contribute to a cleaner future and the Kingdom's Vision 2030 goals of economic diversification and reduced emissions.

Car-sharing platforms enable hosts to generate additional income. Cruiz is committed to helping hosts succeed by providing a trusted marketplace, secure payments and support. In similar P2P platforms, hosts have collectively earned millions of Saudi Riyals. Respectful interactions and adherence to these guidelines ensure that everyone benefits fairly.

If you encounter unsafe behaviour, policy violations or other concerns, contact Cruiz customer support promptly. We work closely with partners and insurers to address issues and continuously improve our platform. Your feedback helps us build a better, more innovative service for all.

By adhering to these community guidelines, you help ensure that Cruiz remains a trusted, safe and sustainable mobility solution. Thank you for being a part of our community.
